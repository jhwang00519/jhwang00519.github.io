Focus Areas

Research Interests

01

Scientific Machine Learning

Neural operator learning for PDEs, data-driven nonlocal constitutive models, deep learning methods for differential equations, theory of physics-guided learning.

02

Numerical Methods for PDEs

High-order numerical schemes, stability and convergence analysis, absorbing boundary conditions, efficient algorithms for partial differential equations on unbounded domains.

03

Nonlocal Models

Peridynamic models and neural operators, nonlocal wave equations, fractional differential equations, material modeling with nonlocal interactions.
